About

What is TVH?

The Tshwane Varsity Hackathon is a hacking competition that is organised by the Tshwane University Of Technology's
faculty of information and communication technology and research and innovation office.

On the day of Hackathon, participants will be requested to come up with solutions to certain real-life problems by using technology.
Each team will be allocated a mentor/coach to work with during the duration of the hackathon.

The Tshwane Varsity Hackathon is open to students from Tshwane University Of Technology.

NO APPLICATION FEE REQUIRED.


Teams will be formed during the workshop day.

What do you need?

You will need your laptop/desktop and internet connection to connect to the online event.
